About Us

Founded by Mythili Kota

A passionate high school student from New Jersey with a vision to transform how teenagers approach food and nutrition.

Co-founded by Sumedh Kota

Co-founder and IT/Website Developer of the non-profit, bringing technical expertise to make food literacy accessible through innovative digital solutions.

At Nourish 'N' Flourish, we believe that healthy eating should be enjoyable, educational, and accessible to everyone. Our 501(c)(3) non-profit organization is dedicated to spreading food literacy among teenagers while preserving the joy and tradition in cooking.
